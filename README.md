# Ink-And-Earth-BETA-
Your mother dies as what seems to be a demon who murders her right infront of your eyes, so you decide to take matters in your own hands and find who killed her, before your mother died, she gave you a massive paintbrush which seems to be magical in a way, you try drawing something, and it comes to life! So you start your adventure in the forest. You find a dark pit which seemingly is a portal to hell, (yikes) so, you jump in after killing your first boss, the rest is for you to find out.
